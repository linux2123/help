GeneralHelp

This section can help with any common and simple to fix issues that people may find when placing a donation, using out website or app, or any other platform that Velvet Charities use.

Payment Issues

If a payment encounters any issues or fails to process successfully, please promptly contact one of our staff members via email. We are committed to ensuring a smooth experience for our supporters, and we will promptly initiate the refund process to address any discrepancies.





Website Issues

If you encounter any issues such as bugs or glitches while navigating our website, we encourage you to report them to our Bug Bounty Program. Your efforts in helping us maintain a seamless user experience are greatly appreciated, and you may even be eligible to receive a reward for your contribution to improving our platform.





App Issues

Should you encounter any glitches or bugs while utilizing our app, we invite you to participate in our Bug Bounty Program by reporting them. Your contributions toward enhancing the performance and reliability of our application are highly valued, and you may qualify for a reward as a token of appreciation for your assistance in refining our platform.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do Velvet Watches keep their prices so low?

We prioritize your affordability by operating on slimmer profit margins, allowing us to maintain quality while offering fair and generous prices. Our commitment is to make innovative watches accessible to everyone.

When was Velvet Watches founded?

Founded just this year, our company emerged with a mission: to counter exorbitant watch prices. Appalled by the industry's high costs, we committed to making quality watches affordable for everyone, ensuring in a new era of accessible timepieces
