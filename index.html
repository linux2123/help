Help

What To Do If Money Doesn't Go Through.

If you encounter any issues with the donation process, such as difficulties in processing payments or any other technical hitches, we encourage you to reach out to our support team via email for assistance. Please send an email to help@velvetcharity.com with details of the problem you're experiencing, and our dedicated support team will promptly assist you in resolving the issue. Your contributions are crucial to our mission, and we're committed to ensuring a seamless and hassle-free donation experience for all our supporters. Thank you for your understanding and patience as we work to address any concerns you may have.

Problems With The Site?

In the event of encountering issues with our website, we prioritize the seamless functionality and security of our platform. If you believe you've identified a potential bug or vulnerability, we encourage you to report it through our Bug Bounty Program for assessment by our dedicated team. However, if the issue does not pertain to a bug but rather concerns usability or general inquiries, please reach out to an administrator on our forums for assistance. Additionally, for any urgent matters or technical difficulties, feel free to contact us directly via email at contact@velvetcharity.com. Your feedback is invaluable in helping us maintain a safe and reliable online environment for all users, and we appreciate your cooperation in keeping our platform running smoothly.

Problems With The App?

In the event of encountering issues with our app, we prioritize swift resolution to ensure a seamless user experience. If you come across any challenges or glitches while using our app, we kindly ask you to reach out to our administrators directly on our forums. Our forum administrators are equipped to provide prompt assistance and can swiftly address any technical issues or concerns you may encounter. Your feedback is instrumental in helping us maintain the quality and reliability of our app, and we greatly appreciate your cooperation in reaching out to our administrators for assistance. Thank you for your understanding and support as we work diligently to address any issues and enhance your user experience.
